- Series Title -
In the past, humanity was nearly driven to extinction by the demons and the Demon King.
But with the arrival of magic, humanity turned the tide, defeated the Demon King, and rose to become the dominant force.

However, the hatred that had built up over countless years led to an order to exterminate the demons completely, sparking resentment among certain groups of people.
Those people were branded as rebels—traitors to humanity.

As the conflict intensified, the rebels were hunted down no differently than the demons themselves.
Living in hiding was not the answer.
They did not simply hide—they came together and fought back.

The world was soon divided into three factions: the Hunters, the Demons, and the Rebels.
Amid a great war, the battle between conflicting ideals began.
